## 📈 How the bot trades

The software uses a process called CLOB automation. It monitors the Central Limit Order Book for changes. When it identifies a setup that matches your criteria, it places an order.

The AI component reviews current news and social trends. It compares this data against market prices. If the sentiment matches your settings, the bot mirrors the actions of successful whale accounts. All trades happen on the blockchain.

**Does the bot store my funds?**
No. The bot only sends instructions to the blockchain. Your funds stay in your wallet at all times.

## 📖 Glossary of terms

*   CLOB: Central Limit Order Book. It lists buy and sell orders.
*   Polygon: The network where prediction markets exist.
*   Whale: A trader with a large balance.
*   Sentiment: The general mood of the market based on news and social posts.
*   HFT: High-Frequency Trading. This refers to fast, automated order execution.
*   API: A link between your account and the software.
